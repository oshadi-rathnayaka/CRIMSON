CRIMSON - Legal Process Handbook (Citizen Edition)

Step 1: File your report with complete details.
Step 2: Preserve evidence and references.
Step 3: Track case updates through the portal.
Step 4: Attend required interviews/hearings.
Step 5: Follow officer/legal guidance for next actions.

Need support?
- Use Support > Legal Aid in CRIMSON.
- Contact emergency services for immediate danger.
